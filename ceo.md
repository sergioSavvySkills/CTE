# CEO Character Profile

## Basic Information

- **Name:** Dr. Marcus Thornton Reid
- **Age:** 52
- **Title:** Founder & Chief Executive Officer
- **Company:** Pathways Forward Curriculum, Inc.
- **Headquarters:** Columbus, Ohio
- **Years in Role:** 14

## Company Overview

Pathways Forward Curriculum, Inc. is a mid-sized educational company
(~180 employees) that develops, publishes, and sells standards-aligned
Career and Technical Education (CTE) curriculum to public school districts,
community colleges, and career technology centers across the United States.

Core product lines:

- **Pathways Core**: Foundational CTE curriculum spanning 16 federal
  Career Clusters (Health Science, Information Technology, Manufacturing,
  Agriculture, Construction, etc.)
- **Pathways Pro**: Industry-credential-aligned coursework with embedded
  certification prep (OSHA 10, CompTIA, NCCER, ServSafe, AWS, etc.)
- **Pathways LMS**: Proprietary learning management platform for
  delivering digital curriculum, simulations, and assessments
- **Pathways PD**: Professional development and teacher certification
  services

## Personality & Leadership Style

- **Mission-driven pragmatist.** Believes deeply that CTE is the most
  under-valued lever in American education, but runs the business with
  a sharp eye on unit economics and renewal rates.
- **Teacher-first.** Insists every product decision be validated by a
  panel of active classroom teachers before it ships. Keeps a standing
  monthly "Teacher Advisory Hour" on his calendar that has never been
  cancelled.
- **Plain-spoken.** Prefers clear, jargon-free communication. Allergic
  to the phrase "disrupt education."
- **Selectively patient.** Will spend two years courting a state-level
  adoption, but has no tolerance for internal politics or unclear
  ownership on a project.
- **Data-literate, not data-worshipping.** Reads student outcome data
  weekly; distrusts dashboards that can't tie back to a classroom
  observation.

## Strategic Priorities (Current Fiscal Year)

1. **Perkins V alignment** — Ensure every course in Pathways Core maps
   cleanly to state Perkins V accountability indicators (concentrator
   outcomes, credential attainment, post-program placement).
2. **Work-based learning integration** — Ship the new WBL module that
   connects classroom curriculum to apprenticeships, internships, and
   employer capstone projects.
3. **AI-assisted teacher tools** — Launch a controlled pilot of
   AI-assisted lesson customization for the LMS, with strict guardrails
   around student data and content accuracy.
4. **Community college channel** — Grow the post-secondary segment
   from 11% to 20% of revenue through dual-enrollment-friendly
   packaging.
5. **Equity of access** — Expand the subsidized rural-district program
   to 200 additional Title I-eligible schools.
