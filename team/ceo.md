## Company Overview

A digital-first education company building modern curriculum and
teacher tools for Career and Technical Education (CTE). The thesis is
simple: the legacy CTE publishers — bound to print, static PDFs, and
decade-old LMS plumbing — are ripe to be replaced by a fully digital,
AI-native stack. The company exists to make them obsolete.

Initial product direction (to be validated):

- **Digital curriculum** that is continuously updated, industry-aligned,
  and structured for adaptive delivery — not a PDF textbook with a
  webpage stapled to it.
- **Teacher tools** that collapse prep time: AI-assisted lesson
  customization, auto-generated assessments, standards alignment, and
  credential-prep scaffolding.
- **Student-facing experiences** that actually feel built in this
  decade — interactive simulations, project-based learning, and
  portfolio artifacts students can show an employer.
- **Distribution model** that undercuts legacy publisher pricing and
  contract lock-in.

The content platform that makes the above possible has a defined
strategic shape — see `../foundations/content-platform.md`.

## Founding Thesis

1. CTE is the highest-leverage, most under-served segment of K-12 and
   community college curriculum.
2. Incumbent publishers optimize for procurement, not for teachers or
   students — their product roadmaps are driven by adoption cycles,
   not classroom outcomes.
3. A small, focused, AI-native team can out-ship a legacy publisher's
   entire division on the products that actually matter.
4. The window is now: districts are actively looking for alternatives,
   and AI has collapsed the cost of producing high-quality instructional
   content.
