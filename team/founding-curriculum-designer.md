# Founding Curriculum Designer

## Basic Information

- **Name:** Maya Delgado
- **Age:** 37
- **Title:** Founding Curriculum Designer
- **Reports to:** Sergio (CEO)
- **Location:** Remote (based in Austin, TX)
- **Start date:** Week 1 of the company
- **Employee #:** 2

## Role Summary

Maya owns curriculum quality, pedagogical approach, and teacher
credibility from day one. She is the person who makes sure every
product decision would pass a real CTE teacher's smell test — and
whose name on the masthead makes districts take the company
seriously.

## Why She's the Right First Hire

1. **She has stood in the classroom.** Nine years teaching CTE means
   she knows what teachers actually need, not what publishers imagine
   they need.
2. **She has seen the legacy sausage get made.** Two years inside a
   major CTE publisher's content team gave her a front-row seat to
   why their output is slow, watered down, and perpetually out of
   date.
3. **She's digital-native about curriculum.** She doesn't think of
   content as "a textbook with a website next to it." She thinks in
   modules, data, and learner pathways.
4. **She's a builder, not a bureaucrat.** Left the publisher
   specifically because she wanted to ship, not manage review cycles.

## Responsibilities (First 90 Days)

1. **Pick the wedge pathway with Sergio.** Pressure-test the IT vs.
   Business vs. Health Science decision against her direct classroom
   experience and current teacher demand signals.
2. **Run the teacher discovery effort.** Own the 30+ teacher
   conversations: recruit participants, design the interview guide,
   synthesize findings into product requirements.
3. **Define the content architecture.** Module shape, assessment
   model, standards-alignment approach, credential-prep integration.
4. **Ship v0 content for the wedge pathway.** One full unit, end-to-
   end, ready for a real teacher to use in a real classroom.
5. **Stand up the Teacher Advisory Panel.** 8–12 active CTE teachers
   on a modest stipend, meeting monthly, reviewing everything before
   it ships.

## Ongoing Ownership

- Curriculum quality bar across all pathways.
- Standards & credential alignment (state CTE frameworks, Perkins V
  accountability indicators, industry certifications).
- Teacher credibility: conference presence, teacher community, the
  "would a real teacher respect this?" gut-check on every feature.
- Recruiting and managing subsequent curriculum designers as pathways
  expand.
