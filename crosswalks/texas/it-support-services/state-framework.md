## Relevant Texas CTE Courses (IT pathway)

Texas organizes IT Support coursework under the **Computers and Technology**
CTE pathway within the **Information Technology** career cluster.

Key courses to ingest:
- Principles of Information Technology (Year 1, Grade 9-10)
- Computer Maintenance (Year 2, Grade 10-11)
- IT Essentials / Practicum in Information Technology (Year 3-4)

## IBC Connection

CompTIA A+, CompTIA ITF+, and Google IT Support Certificate are all on
the Texas 2025–2030 IBC list. State standards must be cross-walked to
the anchor credential objectives to ensure alignment earns IBC funding.
