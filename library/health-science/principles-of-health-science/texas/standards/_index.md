## Lesson-type conventions

- **Dedicated** — owns its own lesson block; appears standalone in a teacher's course calendar.
- **Transversal** — behavior standard reinforced across every lesson; no standalone session. Front-matter carries a `reinforced_by:` list pointing at host standards where the behavior is surfaced.
- **Hybrid** — *reserved; not yet assigned. Use for a standard taught inside another standard's lesson rather than standalone.*
