# Health Science Clinical

**TEKS Code:** 130.204
**Credits:** 2
**Grade Band:** 11–12
**Status:** Planned

## Scope

Hands-on clinical practicum applying theory from 130.203. Students perform clinical skills in simulated or real healthcare settings: vital signs, specimen collection, patient care, and documentation.

## TEKS Standards

To be ingested from TEA Chapter 130 (Health Science Technology).

## Credential Targets

To be determined. Likely: NHA CCMA, NHA CPT, CPR/BLS.

## Notes

- 2-credit course; typically a double-period or block schedule
- Taken concurrently with 130.203 per TEA program-of-study guidance
- Prerequisite: 130.202
