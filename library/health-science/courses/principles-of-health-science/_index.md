# Principles of Health Science

**TEKS Code:** 130.202
**Credits:** 1
**Grade Band:** 9–12
**Status:** Planned

## Scope

Introductory course covering the health science industry, career pathways, safety, infection control, body systems, and foundational clinical skills. Serves as the entry point for the Texas Health Science CTE sequence.

## TEKS Standards

To be ingested from TEA Chapter 130 (Health Science Technology).

## Credential Targets

To be determined. Likely: CPR/BLS, NHA CCMA (partial coverage).

## Notes

- Prerequisite for 130.203 and 130.204
- One semester (18 weeks), 45-minute class periods standard
