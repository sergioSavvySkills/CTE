## Learning objective

Students will be able to diagnose and resolve common physical and connectivity issues on smartphones and tablets.

## Content stub

- **Battery issues:** rapid drain (background apps, screen brightness), swollen battery (safety hazard), won't charge (cable/port/charge IC)
- **Screen and touch issues:** cracked digitizer, touch not registering, ghost touches, liquid damage indicators
- **Connectivity problems:** Wi-Fi dropping (forget/rejoin, reset network settings), Bluetooth won't pair, cellular no service
- **Physical damage:** water damage steps (power off immediately, do not charge, desiccant); dropped-damage triage
- **Performance issues:** clearing app cache, factory reset as last resort, storage full causing slowdown
- **Update and boot issues:** stuck on boot logo (soft reset, recovery mode), update failure (free up storage, stable Wi-Fi required)

## Assessment stub

- Scenario triage: given 6 mobile complaints, list the first two diagnostic steps for each
- Lab: on a practice device, demonstrate clearing app cache, checking battery health, and resetting network settings
- Short answer: a user's phone is hot and the battery drains in 2 hours — list three likely causes
