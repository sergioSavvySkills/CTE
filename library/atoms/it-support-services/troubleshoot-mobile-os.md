## Learning objective

Students will be able to diagnose and resolve software issues on iOS and Android devices including app crashes, update failures, and account sync problems.

## Content stub

- **App crashes:** force-close, clear cache/data, uninstall/reinstall, check for update, check OS compatibility
- **OS update failures:** insufficient storage, failed download (retry on Wi-Fi), update loop (Recovery Mode)
- **Account sync issues:** sign out and back in, check network, reset sync, re-add account
- **Accessibility and display problems:** zoom stuck, inverted colors, screen rotation locked; where to find each setting
- **Factory reset procedures:** Android factory reset steps (Settings and Recovery Mode); iOS erase all content; when to choose each
- **App compatibility:** side-loaded apps, developer mode, managing app permissions post-update

## Assessment stub

- Troubleshoot flowchart: build a decision tree for diagnosing 'app keeps crashing' on Android
- Lab: demonstrate clearing app cache vs. app data on Android and explain the difference
- Short answer: a user's iPhone is stuck in a restart loop after an update — what is the first step?
