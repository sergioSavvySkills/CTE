## Learning objective

Students will be able to create a basic slide presentation with text, images, and transitions using presentation software.

## Content stub

- **Interface overview:** slide panel, editing area, notes pane, view modes (Normal/Slide Sorter/Presenter)
- **Slide layouts:** title slide, title+content, blank; applying and changing layouts; master slide concept
- **Adding content:** text boxes, bullet points, images, icons, shapes; placeholder vs. free text box
- **Themes and design:** applying themes; customizing colors and fonts; keeping design consistent
- **Slide transitions and animations:** slide transitions (Fade, Push); object animations (Appear, Fly In); avoiding overuse
- **File formats and presenting:** saving as .pptx vs. .pdf vs. Google Slides; exporting as video; presenter view controls

## Assessment stub

- Starter presentation: create a 5-slide presentation on any IT topic with title, 3 content slides, and a closing slide
- Short answer: why is it recommended to use a pre-built theme rather than manually formatting every slide?
- Design critique: given a poorly designed slide, identify three specific improvements
