# Digital Technology → Network Systems & Cybersecurity

## Scope (Advance CTE definition)

> *"Careers focused on establishing and managing communication networks
> and protecting them against cyber threats. This Sub-Cluster includes
> network setup, administration, and maintenance and the implementation
> of security measures to prevent unauthorized access and data
> breaches."*

Source: *Guidebook: The Modernized National Career Clusters Framework*,
Advance CTE, April 2025 (p. 5).

**Example programs of study:** Computer Networking, Cybersecurity, IT
Systems.

## Provenance & verification status

- Canonical data lives in `network-systems-cybersecurity.csv` alongside
  this file.
- Researched against **7 states**: CA, TX, FL, NY, PA, OH, IL.
- This is the **most established** Digital Technology sub-cluster for
  CTE credentialing. CompTIA Network+ and Security+ are among the
  most-prepped CTE certs nationally. Cisco CCNA has been a CTE staple
  for 20+ years.
- CompTIA Network+ and Security+ are confirmed across **all 7 states**
  — the strongest cross-state coverage of any credential in any Digital
  Technology sub-cluster.

## Anchor recommendations

**Four-credential anchor stack for a HS Networking & Cybersecurity
pathway (4-year, 9th-12th):**

1. **Cisco CCST Networking + CCST Cybersecurity** *(Year 1-2, 9th-10th)*
   — the entry ramp. Launched 2023 specifically for HS/early career.
   50-minute exams, Certiport-delivered, 5-year validity. OH 6 pts
   each. Steps directly into CCNA or CyberOps Associate. Think of these
   as "the ITF+ of networking/security."

2. **CompTIA Network+** *(Year 2-3, 10th-11th)* — the comprehensive
   networking credential. Vendor-neutral. Confirmed across all 7 states.
   OH 6 pts. The natural prerequisite foundation for everything else in
   this sub-cluster.

3. **CompTIA Security+** *(Year 3-4, 11th-12th)* — the capstone. Most-
   prepped cybersecurity cert in CTE. DoD baseline. Confirmed across
   all 7 states. OH 6 pts. If a student earns Security+ by graduation,
   they are job-ready for entry cybersecurity roles.

**Alternative pathway (Cisco-aligned):**
CCST Networking (9-10th) → CCNA (11th) → Security+ or CyberOps
Associate (12th). This trades CompTIA Network+ for Cisco CCNA — both
are valid, the choice depends on whether the program uses Cisco
Networking Academy curriculum or CompTIA-aligned curriculum.

**Complementary (free, no-cost-to-schools):**
- ISC2 CC — free exam, free training. Low-barrier first cybersecurity
  credential. Great for 9th/10th-graders who aren't ready for CCST yet.
- Google Cybersecurity Certificate — course-completion, not proctored,
  but explicitly prepares students for Security+.

## State research notes

- **California:** No centralized list. CompTIA, Cisco, and vendor-
  neutral certs widely used at district discretion. Marked "yes" where
  widely adopted.

- **Texas:** 2025-2030 IBC list (523 certs) includes CompTIA Network+,
  Security+, CySA+; Cisco CyberOps Associate (200-201); TestOut and
  Certiport historically included. CCST, ISC2 CC, EC-Council CCT need
  verification against full list.

- **Florida:** CAPE list includes Cisco CCST Cybersecurity, CCST
  Networking, CompTIA Security+, Network+, CCNA historically. TestOut
  and Certiport ITS historically present. Excel download needed for
  2025-26 confirmation.

- **New York:** NYSED IT list includes CompTIA Network+ (with A+
  prereq noted), CCNA, CompTIA Security+ (with Network+ prereq noted).
  Law/Public Safety list adds CySA+, EC-Council Ethical Hacking
  Associate, SANS certs. ISC2 CC NOT listed as of Jan 2024 update.
  Lists are notably dated.

- **Pennsylvania:** Broad list. Includes CompTIA Network+, Security+,
  Server+, Linux+; Cisco CCNA, CCST IT Support, CCST Networking, CCST
  Cybersecurity; ISC2 CC; Microsoft MTA variants; TestOut; Certiport
  IC3. One of the broader approved lists.

- **Ohio:** Most granular with point values. CCNA (12 pt), CEH (12 pt),
  CompTIA Network+ (6 pt), Security+ (6 pt), CySA+ (6 pt), CCST
  Networking (6 pt), CCST Cybersecurity (6 pt), CyberOps Associate
  (6 pt), Fortinet NSE 4 (3 pt), ISC2 CC (1 pt). 3-year IRC re-
  evaluation underway.

- **Illinois:** ISBE recommended list includes CompTIA (A+, Security+,
  Network+), Cisco (CCNA). PDF not machine-readable for credential-
  level verification.
