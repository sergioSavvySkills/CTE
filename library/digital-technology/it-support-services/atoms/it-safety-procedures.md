## Learning objective

Students will be able to apply electrical safety practices, handle equipment safely, and identify workplace hazards specific to IT environments.

## Content stub

- **Electrical safety:** never work on live equipment; discharge capacitors (monitors/UPS); ESD wrist straps; GFCI outlets
- **Lifting and ergonomics:** proper lifting technique for heavy servers; two-person lifts; server rail systems; ergonomic workstation setup
- **Toxic materials:** toner powder (respiratory risk), CRT phosphor, battery acid, thermal paste; proper PPE
- **Fire safety:** electrical fire suppression (CO2, FM-200, not water); server room fire suppression systems
- **Cable management:** preventing trip hazards; proper cable routing in racks; strain relief
- **Tools and workspace:** using the right tool for the job; keeping workspace organized; MSDS/SDS sheets

## Assessment stub

- Hazard identification: given a server room photo, identify 5 safety hazards
- PPE selection: for 5 IT tasks, identify what PPE (if any) is appropriate
- Short answer: why should you never use water to extinguish an electrical fire?
