## Learning objective

Students will be able to identify and demonstrate the professional work behaviors and personal qualities that enhance employability and job advancement in an IT environment.

## Content stub

- **Workplace presence:** attendance, punctuality, appropriate attire for the role and setting
- **Workspace habits:** clean and safe work environment, tool care, respect for shared equipment
- **Personal qualities:** pride in work, flexibility, open-mindedness, initiative, willingness to learn new technologies
- **Accountability:** taking ownership of tasks, meeting deadlines, asking for help when needed
- **Scenarios specific to IT support:** tech bench etiquette, handling client equipment, respecting confidentiality during repair

## Assessment stub

- Scenario sort: given 8 workplace situations, students classify each as demonstrating or violating professional work behavior
- Reflection short-answer: students describe two behaviors they would prioritize in their first IT support role and why
- Self-assessment rubric applied to a simulated week of work
