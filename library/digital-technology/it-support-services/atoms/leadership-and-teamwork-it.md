## Learning objective

Students will be able to demonstrate effective leadership behaviors and function as a contributing team member on IT projects and support tasks, including collaboration across diverse backgrounds.

## Content stub

- **Team roles in IT:** help desk tier structure, project teams, on-call rotations, cross-functional work with non-IT stakeholders
- **Leadership without authority:** taking initiative, volunteering for ownership, mentoring peers
- **Collaboration skills:** sharing knowledge, fair workload distribution, constructive feedback
- **Working across cultures:** communicating with team members and customers from different backgrounds, language considerations, respect for varied work norms
- **Conflict resolution:** handling disagreements over technical approaches, escalating appropriately
- **Team tools:** chat, ticketing, shared documentation, stand-ups

## Assessment stub

- Group project: small teams diagnose a simulated multi-system outage; each student rotates through lead and contributor roles, then self-assesses
- Peer review: teammates evaluate each other's contributions using a rubric
- Scenario analysis: given a team-conflict scenario, describe how to resolve it
