## Learning objective

Students will be able to identify organizations working on beneficial and safe AI and describe one current project at each.

## Content stub

- **AI for Good Foundation:** NGO network focused on AI for humanitarian and sustainable development goals
- **Center for Human-Compatible AI:** UC Berkeley research center on aligning AI with human values
- **Future of Life Institute:** grants, papers, and advocacy on long-term AI safety and existential risk
- **Partnership on AI:** industry consortium on responsible AI practices
- **Local orgs:** school-level clubs, hackathons, and student-led AI-for-good chapters
- **How to get involved:** internship, open-source contribution, hackathon, volunteering on data-collection or verification

## Assessment stub

- Research: pick one organization and summarize one current project
- Design: pitch an 'AI for Good' project for your school or community
- Short answer: why is independent, non-corporate AI research important for safety?
