## Learning objective

Students will be able to discuss how AI is reshaping careers, identify new AI-adjacent jobs, and analyze how existing roles are shifting.

## Content stub

- **Jobs AI creates:** prompt engineer, ML engineer, data scientist, AI ethicist, AI auditor, AI product manager, AI trainer
- **Jobs AI transforms:** writers use AI drafting tools, teachers use AI tutoring aides, designers use AI generators as co-pilots
- **Tasks AI automates:** routine document review, first-pass customer support, image tagging, basic coding assistance, scheduling
- **Skills that stay valuable:** judgment, domain expertise, creativity, relationship-building, complex problem-solving, ethical reasoning
- **Preparing now:** learn to direct AI (prompting), evaluate AI output critically, build portfolios that show your AI use

## Assessment stub

- Research: find one career you're interested in — describe 3 ways AI is changing how that work gets done
- Design: plan a 3-year learning path to enter an AI-adjacent role
- Debate: 'AI will eliminate more jobs than it creates' — argue both sides in 3 sentences each
