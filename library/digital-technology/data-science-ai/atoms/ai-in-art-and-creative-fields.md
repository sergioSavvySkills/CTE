## Learning objective

Students will be able to identify how AI is used in creative fields and analyze the ethical debates around AI-generated art.

## Content stub

- **Visual art:** Midjourney, DALL-E, Stable Diffusion — text-to-image; also style transfer and image editing tools
- **Music:** Suno/Udio for composition, AI mastering tools, voice cloning for vocalists, royalty-free track generation
- **Writing and film:** AI screenplay assist, video generation (Sora, Runway), automated editing, color grading
- **Ethical debates:** copyright on training data, style mimicry, artist consent, authenticity, attribution
- **Artists' response:** workflow integration, new art forms (prompt art), guild pushback, licensing frameworks

## Assessment stub

- Experiment: use a free AI image generator to create 3 variations on a theme; compare results
- Analyze: find 2 news stories about AI and creative industries — summarize the positions
- Short answer: is AI-generated art 'real' art? Defend your position in 3 sentences
