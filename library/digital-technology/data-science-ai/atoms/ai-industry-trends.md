## Learning objective

Students will be able to identify current trends in the AI industry and make informed predictions about near-term developments.

## Content stub

- **Current investment trends:** massive capital into foundation-model companies, AI chip (GPU/TPU) shortages, enterprise AI adoption
- **Regulatory trends:** EU AI Act in force, US executive orders and state laws, GDPR + privacy intersections
- **Technical trends:** multimodal models, smaller efficient models (distilled), on-device AI, agents, reasoning models
- **Workforce trends:** mass upskilling, AI literacy in every role, shift from 'AI teams' to 'AI everywhere'
- **Risks on the radar:** disinformation, copyright lawsuits, energy cost, job displacement, concentration of power

## Assessment stub

- Research: name 3 companies driving AI investment right now and what each is betting on
- Predict: write 3 predictions for the next 12 months with your reasoning
- Short answer: what regulatory change do you think will have the biggest impact on students entering AI?
