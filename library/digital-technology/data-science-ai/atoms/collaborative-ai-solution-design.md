## Learning objective

Students will be able to work in a team to design an AI solution using Design Thinking and iterate based on feedback.

## Content stub

- **Divide by skill:** each teammate takes a role (data, modeling, UX, presentation) but shares understanding across all
- **Empathize as a team:** interview users together — different ears catch different things
- **Ideate broadly:** many ideas first; pick the one that best balances impact + feasibility + team strengths
- **Design the AI component:** what specifically does the AI do, with what input and output? where does it fit in the user flow?
- **Fast prototype:** wireframe the app; fake the AI output with hardcoded examples to test user flow first
- **Design reviews:** schedule 2 checkpoints with stakeholders; don't wait until the end for feedback

## Assessment stub

- Lab: in a 3-person team, apply this process to an identified problem; produce a design doc
- Present: 5-minute design review; get feedback from another team
- Short answer: why prototype the user flow with fake AI output before building the real model?
