## Learning objective

Students will be able to describe how AI has been used to solve problems historically and identify current and near-future problem areas where AI delivers value.

## Content stub

- **Historical wins:** chess/Go (game-playing), speech-to-text, OCR on mail, ad targeting, fraud detection
- **Current wins:** medical image diagnosis, drug discovery, protein folding (AlphaFold), language translation, code assistance
- **Near-term wins:** autonomous delivery, personalized tutoring, climate modeling, early-warning systems
- **Where AI still fails:** common-sense reasoning, novel situations, small-data problems, tasks requiring real understanding
- **Pattern:** AI excels at narrow, data-rich tasks with clear success criteria; struggles with broad, sparse-data tasks

## Assessment stub

- Group the 8 example problems by era (past/present/future) and justify
- Short answer: why has AI succeeded at image recognition but struggles with common-sense reasoning?
- Design: propose an AI solution for a problem in your school or community
