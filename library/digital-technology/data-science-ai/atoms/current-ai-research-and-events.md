## Learning objective

Students will be able to find, read at a high level, and summarize current AI research and industry developments.

## Content stub

- **Research sources:** arXiv.org (preprints), Papers With Code, major conferences: NeurIPS, ICML, CVPR, ACL
- **Industry news:** TechCrunch AI, Ars Technica, The Batch (Andrew Ng), Import AI newsletter, MIT Tech Review
- **Reading a paper:** abstract first, then intro + conclusion, then skim methods; don't get bogged down in math early
- **Evaluating credibility:** peer review vs preprint, source reputation, reproducibility, independent replication
- **Tracking trends:** follow specific researchers on social media; subscribe to weekly AI newsletters; set up Google Alerts

## Assessment stub

- Find a recent AI paper on arXiv in a topic you care about; summarize the abstract in plain English
- Compare: find the same AI news story covered in 2 different sources; note tone and framing differences
- Curate: build a list of 5 sources you'll follow weekly for AI news
