## Learning objective

Students will be able to analyze a dataset and construct informed summaries, decisions, or predictions with appropriate caveats.

## Content stub

- **Framing the question:** what decision will this analysis inform? be specific about the outcome
- **Descriptive stats:** mean, median, mode, range, standard deviation — summarize the dataset
- **Comparative analysis:** group-bys and comparisons reveal differences between segments
- **Trend analysis:** smoothing, moving averages, year-over-year — what direction is the metric going?
- **Caveats and uncertainty:** sample size, missing data, outliers, confounders — always flag
- **From analysis to recommendation:** state the finding, then the recommended action, then the uncertainty

## Assessment stub

- Lab: given a school-enrollment dataset, compute 3 summary stats and one actionable finding
- Critique: review a report and identify missing caveats
- Design: write a 1-page analysis for a school-leader decision using the structure above
