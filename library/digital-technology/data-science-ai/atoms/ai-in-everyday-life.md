## Learning objective

Students will be able to identify AI in common apps and services and explain what problem each AI is intended to solve.

## Content stub

- **Social media:** feed ranking, content moderation, friend suggestions, auto-captioning, deepfake detection
- **Messaging and email:** spam filtering, smart replies, autocomplete, autocorrect, voice-to-text
- **Shopping and media:** product recommendations, price prediction, Netflix/Spotify rankings, personalized ads
- **Navigation and travel:** route optimization (Google Maps), rideshare matching, flight-price forecasting
- **Assistants:** Siri, Alexa, Google Assistant, Copilot — voice + intent understanding
- **Camera and photos:** portrait mode, scene recognition, photo search by content, object erasure

## Assessment stub

- Audit: list 6 apps on your phone or computer; for each, identify whether it uses AI and what problem the AI solves
- Short answer: how does a recommender system differ from a simple 'most popular' list?
