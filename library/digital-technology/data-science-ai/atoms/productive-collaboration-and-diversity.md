## Learning objective

Students will be able to collaborate productively on team projects and explain why diverse perspectives make teams stronger.

## Content stub

- **Team roles and rotation:** every team needs facilitation, note-taking, and building; rotate so everyone practices each
- **Norms of collaboration:** listen to understand, build on others' ideas, disagree with the idea not the person, finish your commitments
- **Why diversity helps:** different backgrounds catch different blind spots — especially important for AI teams (bias detection)
- **Conflict handling:** surface disagreements early; restate the other person's view before rebutting; focus on shared goals
- **Remote and async:** clear written updates, recorded decisions, documented handoffs — harder but possible
- **Leadership without authority:** influence through preparation, asking good questions, helping others succeed

## Assessment stub

- Role-play: 3-person team disagrees on project direction; practice one conflict-handling technique
- Reflect: describe a team experience where diversity caught a problem you wouldn't have
- Short answer: how does a lack of diversity on an AI team translate into biased AI?
