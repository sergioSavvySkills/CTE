# Foundations of Artificial Intelligence (GA 11.44300)

First course in Georgia's AI CTAE pathway. Introduces AI concepts,
history, applications, intro programming in Python, data fundamentals,
AI ethics, and Design Thinking. Each atom is a 45-minute period;
block-schedule teachers combine two atoms per day.
