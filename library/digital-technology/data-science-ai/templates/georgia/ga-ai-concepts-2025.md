# Artificial Intelligence Concepts (GA 11.44400)

Second course in Georgia's AI CTAE pathway. Deepens ML understanding
(supervised/unsupervised/reinforcement), introduces OOP programming
for AI, data science with DataFrames + SQL + statistics, AI ethics
and policy, and applying Design Thinking to real-world AI problems.
Prereq: Foundations of AI (11.44300). Each atom is 45 minutes.
