# Texas Health Science CTE — Taxonomy

## Focus

This project is focused on the **Texas Health Science** CTE pathway, organized
around Texas TEKS course codes (Chapter 130) rather than the national career
cluster framework.

Texas reference: TEA CTE Health Science Technology, 19 TAC Chapter 130,
Subchapter E — Health Science Technology.

## Texas Health Science Course Sequence

| Course | TEKS Code | Credits | Role |
|--------|-----------|---------|------|
| Principles of Health Science | 130.202 | 1 | Entry (required) |
| Health Science Theory | 130.203 | 1 | Core theory |
| Health Science Clinical | 130.204 | 2 | Core practicum |

Additional specialty courses (dental, pharmacy tech, etc.) added as needed.

## Texas IBC Connection

Texas uses an **Incentive-Based Credential (IBC)** system. Target credentials
for this pathway are confirmed against the current Texas IBC list for Health
Science Technology. See `library/health-science/credentials/_index.csv`.

## Legacy National Framework Note

The national Advance CTE framework (14 clusters, 72 sub-clusters) maps legacy
"Health Science" → "Healthcare & Human Services". Texas CTE data and TEA
frameworks are still organized under the legacy "Health Science" cluster name.
This project uses Texas TEA terminology throughout.
