## (a) General requirements

Students shall be awarded one credit for successful completion of this course.

## (b) Introduction

This course is designed to provide an overview of the five health science
career pathways — therapeutic services, diagnostic services, health informatics,
support services, and biotechnology research and development — with foundational
content in medical terminology, anatomy, professional practice, safety, and
the U.S. health care system.
