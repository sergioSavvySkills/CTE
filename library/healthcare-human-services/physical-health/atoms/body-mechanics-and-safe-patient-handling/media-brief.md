## Accessibility requirements

- Alt text on each asset names the specific body-mechanics or
  equipment concept depicted (not generic "person lifting")
- In m01, the stable and unstable stances must differ in line weight
  or label position as well as color, so the distinction survives
  grayscale printing
- Video assets (m03, m04) require closed captions and an audio-
  descriptive narration track for each step
- Text labels minimum 14 pt, high-contrast against background
- Body-mechanics demo videos should depict workers of varied body
  sizes; the mechanics apply regardless of worker size

## Reuse notes

- **m01** (center-of-gravity / base-of-support diagram) reuses in any
  atom that covers balance, ambulation, or fall prevention
- **m05** (lift-equipment decision flow) reuses in fall-prevention and
  general patient-transfer atoms
- **m06** (equipment icon set) reuses across any atom that references
  patient-handling equipment — avoids re-drawing identical icons
- **m03** and **m04** (transfer demos) are likely to be referenced by
  any clinical-skills atom that includes patient mobility steps
