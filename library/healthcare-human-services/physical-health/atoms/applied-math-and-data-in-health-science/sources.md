# Sources — applied-math-and-data-in-health-science

## Standards and guidelines

### ISMP-Decimal

- **Citation:** Institute for Safe Medication Practices. *ISMP List of Error-Prone Abbreviations, Symbols, and Dose Designations*.
- **URL:** https://www.ismp.org/recommendations/error-prone-abbreviations-list
- **Used for:** decimal-placement errors (trailing zero, missing leading zero), "do not use" dose designations
- **Confidence:** high — national medication-safety authority

### TJC-DoNotUse

- **Citation:** The Joint Commission. *Official "Do Not Use" List of Abbreviations*.
- **URL:** https://www.jointcommission.org/-/media/tjc/documents/standards/national-patient-safety-goals/do_not_use_list.pdf
- **Used for:** trailing-zero and leading-zero rules, accrediting-body expectations
- **Confidence:** high — U.S. healthcare accrediting body

- **Citation:** Centers for Disease Control and Prevention. *CDC Growth Charts*.
- **URL:** https://www.cdc.gov/growthcharts/
- **Used for:** percentile-chart interpretation, pediatric reference ranges
- **Confidence:** high

### NIH-Metric

- **Citation:** National Institute of Standards and Technology. *The International System of Units (SI)*.
- **URL:** https://www.nist.gov/pml/owm/metric-si
- **Used for:** canonical SI (metric) definitions and healthcare standardization
- **Confidence:** high
