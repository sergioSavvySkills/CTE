# Sources — ethics-of-technology-in-healthcare

Every bracketed citation in this atom's `book.md`, `examples.md`, and
`misconceptions.md` resolves to one of the keys below.

## Textbooks

### Beauchamp-Childress

- **Citation:** Beauchamp, T. L., & Childress, J. F. (2019). *Principles of
  Biomedical Ethics* (8th ed.). Oxford University Press.
- **Used for:** the four-principles framework (autonomy, beneficence,
  non-maleficence, justice) applied throughout the atom
- **Confidence:** high — standard bioethics reference, peer-reviewed

## Standards and guidelines

### WHO-AIEthics

- **Citation:** World Health Organization. (2021). *Ethics and Governance
  of Artificial Intelligence for Health: WHO Guidance*.
- **URL:** https://www.who.int/publications/i/item/9789240029200
- **Used for:** bias in diagnostic algorithms, accountability when AI is
  wrong, consent when AI drives decisions
- **Confidence:** high — international public-health body

### ATA-Telehealth

- **Citation:** American Telemedicine Association. *Core Operational
  Guidelines for Telehealth Services*.
- **URL:** https://www.americantelemed.org/resources/
- **Used for:** cross-state licensure, remote exam quality, telehealth
  access and digital-divide framing
- **Confidence:** high — professional society guidance

### Hastings-EOL

- **Citation:** The Hastings Center. *Reports on end-of-life care and
  technology*.
- **URL:** https://www.thehastingscenter.org/
- **Used for:** ethical questions around ventilators, ECMO, artificial
  nutrition, and advance directives
- **Confidence:** high — leading bioethics research institute

## Government / regulatory pages

### GINA-Law

- **Citation:** U.S. Equal Employment Opportunity Commission. *The Genetic
  Information Nondiscrimination Act of 2008* (Pub. L. 110-233).
- **URL:** https://www.eeoc.gov/statutes/genetic-information-nondiscrimination-act-2008
- **Used for:** what GINA covers (health insurance, employment) and what
  it does not cover (life, disability, long-term care insurance)
- **Confidence:** high — U.S. federal statute, EEOC page

### FDA-AIML

- **Citation:** U.S. Food and Drug Administration. *Artificial
  Intelligence and Machine Learning (AI/ML)-Enabled Medical Devices*.
- **URL:** https://www.fda.gov/medical-devices/software-medical-device-samd/artificial-intelligence-and-machine-learning-aiml-enabled-medical-devices
- **Used for:** regulation of AI diagnostic tools, premarket review,
  post-market monitoring
- **Confidence:** high — U.S. federal regulator

### NIH-PrivacyGenomics

- **Citation:** National Human Genome Research Institute / NIH *All of
  Us Research Program*. *Privacy in Genomics*.
- **URL:** https://www.genome.gov/about-genomics/policy-issues/Privacy
- **Used for:** genetic data ownership, family implications of
  individual testing, research-use consent
- **Confidence:** high — U.S. federal research institute
