# Sources — cultural-competence-in-healthcare

## Peer-reviewed articles

### Tervalon-Humility

- **Citation:** Tervalon, M., & Murray-García, J. (1998). Cultural humility versus cultural competence: a critical distinction in defining physician training outcomes in multicultural education. *Journal of Health Care for the Poor and Underserved*, 9(2), 117-125.
- **URL:** https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/10073197/
- **Used for:** the foundational distinction between cultural competence and cultural humility; lifelong self-reflection framing
- **Confidence:** high — peer-reviewed, widely cited foundational article

### IOM-Unequal

- **Citation:** Institute of Medicine (now National Academy of Medicine). (2003). *Unequal Treatment: Confronting Racial and Ethnic Disparities in Health Care*. National Academies Press.
- **URL:** https://nap.nationalacademies.org/catalog/12875/unequal-treatment-confronting-racial-and-ethnic-disparities-in-health-care
- **Used for:** clinician-level bias as a documented contributor to disparate care; systems-plus-clinician framing
- **Confidence:** high — National Academy of Medicine consensus report

## Standards and guidelines

### CLAS-Standards

- **Citation:** U.S. Department of Health and Human Services, Office of Minority Health. *National Standards for Culturally and Linguistically Appropriate Services (CLAS) in Health and Health Care*.
- **URL:** https://thinkculturalhealth.hhs.gov/clas
- **Used for:** naming the CLAS framework as the U.S. standard for culturally and linguistically responsive care
- **Confidence:** high — federal standards body

### AHRQ-Interpreter

- **Citation:** Agency for Healthcare Research and Quality. *Improving Patient Safety Systems for Patients With Limited English Proficiency: A Guide for Hospitals*.
- **URL:** https://www.ahrq.gov/health-literacy/professional-training/lepguide/index.html
- **Used for:** evidence-based practices for working with qualified medical interpreters; risks of using family as interpreters
- **Confidence:** high — federal health-services research agency
