## Accessibility requirements

- Alt text on each asset names the specific protocol step or code
  being depicted (e.g., "RACE step 3: Contain — door closed, oxygen
  shutoff valve labeled"), not generic descriptions
- m05 (codes reference card) must not rely on color alone — each
  code gets a short text label and an icon, so the card works in
  grayscale and for color-blind users
- Video assets (m03) require closed captions and an audio-descriptive
  narration track for each step
- The disclaimer on m05 ("codes vary by facility; confirm your local
  list") must be visually prominent, not a footnote
- Text labels minimum 14 pt, high contrast against background

## Reuse notes

- **m01** (RACE poster) reuses in any workplace-safety atom that
  touches on fire response
- **m04** (evacuation geometry map) reuses in fall-prevention,
  disaster-preparedness, and patient-transport atoms
- **m05** (emergency-codes card) reuses in workplace-violence and
  active-shooter-awareness atoms as a shared reference
- **m02** (PASS sequence) is a generic workplace-safety asset with
  broad reuse outside healthcare
