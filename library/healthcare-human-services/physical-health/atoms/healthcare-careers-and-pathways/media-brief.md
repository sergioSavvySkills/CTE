## Accessibility requirements

- Alt text on each asset names the specific pathways framework
  shown (e.g., "six career families sector map," "four-rung
  education ladder with example roles"), not generic phrasing
- Diagrams must differentiate categories by both color and
  shape/label so distinctions survive grayscale printing and
  common color-vision differences
- The m06 video requires closed captions and an audio-description
  track; on-screen text overlays must meet minimum contrast ratios
- Salary bars in m03 must be labeled as ranges ("order of magnitude,
  look up current figures in the OOH"), never as point estimates
- Role imagery in any human photos should reflect varied ages,
  genders, and backgrounds; avoid imagery that implies certain
  families are gender-coded

## Reuse notes
