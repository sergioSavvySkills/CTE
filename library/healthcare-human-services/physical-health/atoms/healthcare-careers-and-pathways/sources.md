# Sources — healthcare-careers-and-pathways

## Government / regulatory pages

### BLS-OOH

- **Citation:** U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics. *Occupational Outlook Handbook, Healthcare Occupations*.
- **URL:** https://www.bls.gov/ooh/healthcare/
- **Used for:** current typical pay ranges, education-level requirements, and projected growth for each healthcare occupation; framing of salary and job-outlook bands
- **Confidence:** high — U.S. federal labor-statistics agency, updated annually
- **Access date:** 2026-04-21

### HRSA-Workforce

- **Citation:** Health Resources and Services Administration. *Health Workforce Data and Research*.
- **URL:** https://data.hrsa.gov/
- **Used for:** aging-population and underserved-area demand signals that shape the healthcare job outlook; shortage designations
- **Confidence:** high — U.S. federal health-workforce agency
- **Access date:** 2026-04-21

## Standards and guidelines

### NCSBN-Pathways

- **Citation:** National Council of State Boards of Nursing. *Nursing Pathways and Licensure Overview*.
- **URL:** https://www.ncsbn.org/nursing-regulation/education.page

## Other authoritative pages

### AAMC-Careers

- **Citation:** Association of American Medical Colleges. *Careers in Medicine*.
- **URL:** https://www.aamc.org/cim/
- **Used for:** shape of the physician pathway (undergraduate, medical school, residency, fellowship) and the adjacent physician-assistant and advanced-practice provider routes
- **Confidence:** high — national medical-school association

### OnetOnline

- **Citation:** O*NET OnLine. *Occupational Profiles: Healthcare Practitioners and Technical Occupations; Healthcare Support Occupations*.
- **URL:** https://www.onetonline.org/
- **Used for:** interest-to-career mapping via O*NET Interest Profiler codes, family groupings of related occupations, typical education-level tags
- **Confidence:** high — U.S. Department of Labor occupational-information system
