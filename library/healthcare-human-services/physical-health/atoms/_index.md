# Atoms — Physical Health

**Status:** Principles of Health Science coverage complete (22 atoms, draft).

Anatomy and Physiology, Health Science Theory/Clinical, Practicum, etc.) are

## Scope

State-neutral competency atoms shared by physical-health courses. The Texas
course view at
`library/healthcare-human-services/texas/courses/principles-of-health-science/`
references these atoms via `atoms.md` and will link to TEKS via a forthcoming
crosswalk. Atoms are reusable by other state views (Georgia, Florida, etc.)
when added.

## Anchor credentials

Principles of Health Science is a Level 1 foundation course with no direct
credential anchor. Credential objectives attach to Level 3-4 courses
(CNA, EMT, Medical Assistant, etc.) which build on these atoms.
