# Sources — electronic-health-records-basics

Every bracketed citation in this atom's `book.md`, `examples.md`, and
`misconceptions.md` resolves to one of the keys below.

## Standards and guidelines

### TJC-Documentation

- **Citation:** The Joint Commission. *Record of Care, Treatment, and
  Services (RC) standards* — documentation requirements for accredited
  hospitals.
- **URL:** https://www.jointcommission.org/standards/
- **Used for:** expectations for time-stamped, signed, legible chart
  entries and the "correct-not-erase" rule for errors
- **Confidence:** high — accrediting body for U.S. hospitals

### ISMP-CopyPaste

- **Citation:** Institute for Safe Medication Practices. *Guidance on
  the use of copy-paste in electronic health records.*
- **URL:** https://www.ismp.org/resources/copy-and-paste-ehrs-creates-problems
- **Used for:** error patterns from copy-paste in the EHR, the "carrying
  forward stale information" harm pattern
- **Confidence:** high — independent non-profit, widely cited by
  accreditors and professional bodies

## Government / regulatory pages

### ONC-EHR

- **Citation:** Office of the National Coordinator for Health
  Information Technology (HHS). *Health IT Basics — Benefits of EHRs.*
- **URL:** https://www.healthit.gov/topic/health-it-basics/benefits-ehrs
- **Used for:** the EHR-vs-EMR distinction, meaningful-use history,
  shareable-across-providers framing, interoperability goals
- **Confidence:** high — U.S. federal health IT coordinator

### ONC-PatientAccess

- **Citation:** Office of the National Coordinator for Health
  Information Technology (HHS). *Information Blocking and Patient
  Access* (21st Century Cures Act rules).
- **URL:** https://www.healthit.gov/topic/information-blocking
- **Used for:** patient portal rights, the patient's role in reviewing
  their own record, information-blocking prohibitions
- **Confidence:** high — federal regulator

### HIPAA-Security

- **Citation:** U.S. Department of Health and Human Services. *HIPAA
  Security Rule — summary of access controls and audit log requirements.*
- **URL:** https://www.hhs.gov/hipaa/for-professionals/security/
- **Used for:** unique-user access, audit-trail requirement, role-based
  permission framing (treated at the "every chart view is logged" level,
  not regulation-specifics — those belong to the HIPAA atom)
- **Confidence:** high — federal regulator

### AHRQ-EHR-Safety

- **Citation:** Agency for Healthcare Research and Quality. *EHR and
  Health IT Patient Safety* topic hub (includes SAFER Guides and EHR-
  related case collections).
- **URL:** https://www.ahrq.gov/topic/health-it-safety.html
- **Used for:** wrong-patient documentation risk, alert-fatigue research,
  EHR error case framing
- **Confidence:** high — U.S. government health-research agency
