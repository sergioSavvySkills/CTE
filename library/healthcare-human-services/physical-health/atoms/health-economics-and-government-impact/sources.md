# Sources — health-economics-and-government-impact

## Government / regulatory pages

### CMS-NHE

- **Citation:** Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services, Office of the Actuary. *National Health Expenditure Data* (annual series).
- **URL:** https://www.cms.gov/data-research/statistics-trends-and-reports/national-health-expenditure-data
- **Used for:** U.S. national health expenditure as a share of GDP; year-over-year spending totals; payer-mix framing (Medicare, Medicaid, private, out-of-pocket)
- **Confidence:** high — federal statistical series maintained by the CMS Office of the Actuary
- **Access date:** 2026-04-21

### BLS-OOH

- **Citation:** U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics. *Occupational Outlook Handbook* — Healthcare Occupations overview.
- **URL:** https://www.bls.gov/ooh/healthcare/home.htm
- **Used for:** order-of-magnitude U.S. healthcare employment figures; composition of the healthcare workforce across occupations
- **Confidence:** high — federal statistical agency, updated annually
- **Access date:** 2026-04-21

### CMS-ValueBased

- **Citation:** Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services, Innovation Center. *Value-Based Care and Alternative Payment Models* overview pages.
- **URL:** https://www.cms.gov/priorities/innovation/key-concepts/value-based-care
- **Used for:** definitions of value-based care and bundled payments at the framework level (not policy mechanics); how CMS uses payment design to influence practice
- **Confidence:** high — federal regulator and payer
- **Access date:** 2026-04-21

### HRSA-Overview

- **Citation:** Health Resources and Services Administration. *About HRSA* and *Programs and Services* overview pages.
- **URL:** https://www.hrsa.gov/about
- **Used for:** federal role in safety-net care, community health centers, rural and underserved workforce programs
- **Confidence:** high — federal agency
- **Access date:** 2026-04-21

## Standards and guidelines

### NACCHO-LHD

- **Citation:** National Association of County and City Health Officials. *National Profile of Local Health Departments* (periodic survey report).
- **URL:** https://www.naccho.org/resources/lhd-research/national-profile-of-local-health-departments
- **Used for:** scope of local health department activity — food and environmental inspection, immunization, communicable-disease response, school and maternal-child health
- **Confidence:** high — authoritative professional association survey of U.S. local health departments
- **Access date:** 2026-04-21

## Other authoritative pages

### KFF-Medicaid

- **Citation:** KFF (Kaiser Family Foundation). *Medicaid 101* and state-level Medicaid briefs.
- **URL:** https://www.kff.org/medicaid/
- **Used for:** state-federal shared administration of Medicaid; variation in state Medicaid choices; order-of-magnitude enrollment context
- **Confidence:** high — widely cited independent health-policy research organization
- **Access date:** 2026-04-21

### CBO-HealthSpending

- **Citation:** Congressional Budget Office. *The Budget and Economic Outlook* and health-spending projection reports.
- **URL:** https://www.cbo.gov/topics/health-care
- **Used for:** historical context for rising share of GDP devoted to health; federal outlay trends for Medicare and Medicaid
- **Confidence:** high — nonpartisan legislative-branch analytic agency
- **Access date:** 2026-04-21
