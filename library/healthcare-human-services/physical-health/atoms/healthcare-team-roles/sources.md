## Standards and guidelines

### IPEC-Core

- **Citation:** Interprofessional Education Collaborative. *Core Competencies for Interprofessional Collaborative Practice: Version 3* (2023).
- **URL:** https://www.ipecollaborative.org/ipec-core-competencies
- **Used for:** definition of interprofessional team-based care, the four competency domains (values/ethics, roles/responsibilities, communication, teams/teamwork), and why role clarity matters for team performance
- **Confidence:** high — consensus framework from major health-professions education associations
- **Access date:** 2026-04-21

### NCSBN-Delegation

- **Citation:** National Council of State Boards of Nursing. *National Guidelines for Nursing Delegation* (2019).
- **URL:** https://www.ncsbn.org/public-files/NGND-PosPaper_06.pdf
- **Used for:** the "five rights of delegation," the RN-to-CNA/LPN delegation pattern, and what tasks can and cannot be delegated within the nursing ladder
- **Confidence:** high — national nursing regulatory body
- **Access date:** 2026-04-21

### AHRQ-TeamSTEPPS

- **Citation:** Agency for Healthcare Research and Quality. *TeamSTEPPS Program*.
- **URL:** https://www.ahrq.gov/teamstepps-program/
- **Used for:** evidence that team-based communication (including SBAR handoffs) reduces errors, and the definition of SBAR as a standardized handoff format
- **Confidence:** high — federal healthcare-quality agency; TeamSTEPPS is the national standard team-training curriculum
- **Access date:** 2026-04-21
