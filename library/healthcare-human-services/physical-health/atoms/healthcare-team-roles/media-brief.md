## Accessibility requirements

- Alt text on m01 must name the five role categories in order
  (physicians/APPs, nursing ladder, allied health, support/admin,
  behavioral/social), not generic "healthcare team" phrasing
- Diagrams differentiate categories by both color and shape or
  label so distinctions survive grayscale printing and common
  color-vision differences
- The m06 video requires closed captions and an audio-description
  track; on-screen SBAR-segment labels must meet minimum contrast
  ratios
- Role-icon imagery should reflect varied ages, genders, and
  backgrounds; avoid visual cues that gender-code specific roles
  (e.g., nurses as women, physicians as men)
- The "can do / cannot do" labels on m02 must be visually
  balanced so neither side reads as a judgment of the role —
  narrower scope is not lesser work
