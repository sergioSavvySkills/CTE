## Accessibility requirements

- Alt text on each asset names the specific conflict concept
  depicted (e.g., "two-by-two grid of the five conflict styles"),
  not generic phrasing like "chart of styles"
- Diagrams must differentiate categories by both color and
  shape/label so distinctions survive grayscale printing and
  common color-vision differences
- The video in m04 requires closed captions and an audio
  description track; role-play actors should reflect varied ages,
  genders, and backgrounds
- Text labels at minimum 14 pt with high-contrast background
- Avoid any imagery that implies a particular patient population
  is more likely to be "difficult" — the de-escalation skills
  apply universally

## Reuse notes
