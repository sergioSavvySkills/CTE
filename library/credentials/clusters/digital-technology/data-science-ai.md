# Digital Technology → Data Science & Artificial Intelligence

## Scope (Advance CTE definition)

> *"Careers combining the power of data analysis, machine learning, and
> artificial intelligence (AI), including key processes such as data
> modeling and natural language processing. Professionals in this field
> apply critical thinking to work on innovative solutions to interpret
> vast amounts of information, automate decisionmaking processes,
> develop intelligent algorithms that improve with experience, and
> enhance communication between humans and machines. AI requires
> attention to ethical standards and is poised to revolutionize all
> industries by enhancing efficiency, personalizing experiences, and
> driving innovation."*

Source: *Guidebook: The Modernized National Career Clusters Framework*,
Advance CTE, April 2025 (p. 5).

**Example program of study** (Advance CTE): Applied Data Science &
Analytics.

## Provenance & verification status

- Canonical data lives in `data-science-ai.csv` alongside this file.
- Researched against **7 states**: CA, TX, FL, NY, PA, OH, IL.
- This sub-cluster is newer than IT Support — many credentials launched
  2022–2025 and may not yet appear on state lists. `unknown` and
  `verify` markers are common and expected.
- Ohio has the best state-list coverage for this sub-cluster. California
  is decentralized (no single state list). TX and FL lists require
  manual Excel download to confirm individual credentials.

## Anchor recommendations

**Three-credential anchor stack for a HS Data Science & AI pathway:**

1. **Certiport IT Specialist: Data Analytics + AI (stackable pair)** —
   9th/10th-grade entry. Two ~$100 exams, 50 min each, SAS-endorsed,
   designed for HS. Ohio already recognizes both. These are the
   accessibility play — low cost, low barrier, clear scope.

2. **CompTIA Data+ (DA0-002)** — 11th/12th-grade flagship. Vendor-neutral,
   ANSI/ISO accredited, covers the full data analytics lifecycle
   (concepts, acquisition, analysis, visualization, governance). V2 adds
   AI and cloud content. ~$125 with academic discount. The job-ready
   credential.

3. **Microsoft Azure AI Fundamentals (AI-900 → AI-901)** — parallel/senior
   option for the AI side specifically. Free vouchers make cost a
   non-issue. Covers AI/ML concepts, computer vision, NLP, generative AI.
   The AI-900 → AI-901 transition in mid-2026 adds GenAI content.

**Honorable mention:** AWS Certified AI Practitioner (AIF-C01). Launched
August 2024, $100 exam, AWS Educate for ages 13+. Watch state-list
adoption over the next 12 months.

## State research notes

- **California:** No centralized approved IBC list. CTE credential
  approval is decentralized at the district/LEA/regional-consortium
  level. ICT sector includes Data Science & AI pathways, but specific
  credentials are a local decision.

- **Texas:** TEA published the 2025–2030 IBC list with 523
  certifications (Excel + PDF at tea.texas.gov). CompTIA and Certiport
  have historically appeared. Requires manual Excel download to confirm
  DS/AI-specific entries. Marked `verify`.

- **Florida:** CAPE Industry Certification Funding List for 2025–26 is
  available as an Excel download at fldoe.org. CompTIA and Certiport
  have historically appeared. Requires manual download to confirm.
  Marked `verify`.

- **New York:** Decentralized model — credentials approved
  program-by-program, not via a statewide master list. NYSED IT
  credential page lists Python Institute and a few others but is
  missing most modern data/AI credentials. DS/AI pathways are emerging.

- **Pennsylvania:** PDE uses NOCTI as primary credentialing partner.
  Modern data science/AI credentials are largely absent from the
  approved list. PA appears behind in adding newer credentials.

- **Ohio:** Most transparent and detailed IRC list of the seven states,
  organized by career field with point values (1–12 pts). IT list
  includes: MS Azure AI Fundamentals (4 pt), MS Azure Data
  Fundamentals (4 pt), Certiport IT Specialist Python (1 pt), PCEP
  (3 pt), IBM badges (1 pt each), Tableau Desktop (4 pt), MS Power BI
  (6 pt). **Best DS/AI coverage of the seven states.**

- **Illinois:** ISBE maintains an Industry-Recognized Credentials
  Running List PDF, but it returned a 503 error during research.
  CompTIA credentials are known to be included. Specific DS/AI
  approvals could not be confirmed.
